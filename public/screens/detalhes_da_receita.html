Honey Glazed Salmon

star 4.9
schedule TIME 25 min
local_fire_department CALORIES 420 kcal
restaurant DIFFICULTY Easy

Ingredients

2 Servings

Instructions

1

Prepare the Glaze

In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and a splash of lemon juice until well combined.

2

Marinate Salmon

Place salmon fillets in a shallow dish. Pour half of the glaze over the salmon, ensuring it's evenly coated. Let it sit for 10 minutes.

3

Sear the Fillets

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add a dash of olive oil. Sear the salmon skin-side down for 4 minutes, then flip and cook for another 3 minutes.

4

Glaze and Serve

Pour the remaining glaze into the skillet. Let it bubble and thicken for 1 minute, spooning it over the salmon. Serve immediately with fresh parsley.
